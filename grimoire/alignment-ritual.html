Daily Alignment Ritual

30 June 2022 CE

I - The Body of Light

Stand, facing East. Hold the wand in both hands, left over right, pointing upwards. Close your eyes and take as long as you need to put away the outside world and be present in the moment. Be sure to keep good posture and take slow, full breaths.

Visualize a point of energy between your feet that expands into a large, flat disk. At its full size, it should be large enough to completely contain your body. Slowly pull the disk upwards, keeping its flat shape. As it moves up, let it transform your body into one of pure light.

Once the disk has passed above your head, stop its upward motion. Shrink the disk back to a single point and let it disappear. Your body should be made entirely of light. Take a few moments to adjust to this new feeling, and remember to keep your breathing slow and regular.

II - Building the Orb, East Pillar

Hold the wand in your dominant hand. Channel your Magick through the wand to trace a large unicursal octogram before you.

Take a moment to build up Magick in the wand. Step forward with the left foot and push forward, releasing your Magick into the octogram.

When finished, place your hands over the octogram. Push the energy to the right, shifting your position to the South. Your right foot should be forward.

You should now have one octogram to the East, with a quarter-sphere of energy filling the space between the East and the South.

III - Building the Orb, South Pillar

Hold the wand in your dominant hand. Channel your Magick through the wand to trace a large unicursal octogram before you.

Take a moment to build up Magick in the wand. Step forward with the left foot and push forward, releasing your Magick into the octogram.

When finished, place your hands over the octogram. Push the energy to the right, shifting your position to the West. Your right foot should be forward.

You should now have two Octograms, one East and one South. There should be a half-sphere of energy filling the space between the East, South and West.

IV - Building the Orb, West Pillar

Hold the wand in your non-dominant hand. Channel your Magick through the wand to trace a large unicursal octogram before you.

Take a moment to build up Magick in the wand. Step forward with the left foot and push forward, releasing your Magick into the octogram.

When finished, place your hands over the octogram. Push the energy to the right, shifting your position to the North. Your right foot should be forward.

You should now have three octograms: one East, one South, and one West. There should be a three-quarter sphere of energy filling the space between the East, South, West, and North.

V - Building the Orb, North Pillar

Hold the wand in your non-dominant hand. Channel your Magick through the wand to trace a large unicursal octogram before you.

Take a moment to build up Magick in the wand. Step forward with the left foot and push forward, releasing your Magick into the octogram.

When finished, place your hands over the octogram. Push the energy to the right, shifting your position to the East. Your right foot should be forward.

You should now have four octograms: one East, one South, one West, and one North. There should be a full sphere of energy surrounding you, anchored by the four octograms.

VI - Cycling the Essence

Face East. Hold the wand in both hands, pointing upward at stomach level. Let your Magick expand to fill the space.

When ready, Open the Channel again. When the column reaches the top of the Orb, let it mix with its boundary, fortifying the Orb and exiting on the opposite end.

Let the energy from the Channel fill the space of the Orb. Once submerged in Transcendence, take a few moments to adjust to the feeling. It should permeate your being and cleanse your Magick.

VII - Closing the Channel

When ready, disconnect the Column from the Infinite Above. The remaining energy should finish its descent and merge with the Orb.

Drain the Orb of excess energy to the Infinite Below. Once the space is empty, dissolve the Orb. Start from the uppermost point and let it drain towards the Infinite Below as well.

Once finished, your Magick should glow with a white hue around the edges. The space around should also be slightly brighter.
